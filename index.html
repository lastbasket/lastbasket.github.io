Yiming HUANG

I'm a second-year PhD student in the Department of Electronic Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong , advised by Prof. Hongliang Ren. In 2023, I obtained my B.Eng. in the Department of CSE, HKUST.

My research interest lies in Surgical Robotics and 3D Computer Vision.

If you are interested in working with us (in person or remotely) as an intern, please feel free to drop me an email.
